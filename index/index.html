Covid 19/Corona Virus

Symptoms

Most common symptoms include fever, dry cough and tiredness.Less common symptoms: aches and pains sore throat diarrhoea conjunctivitis headache loss of taste or smell a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes. Serious symptoms include difficulty breathing or shortness of breath chest pain or pressure loss of speech or movement.

Treatment

Self care: If you feel sick you should rest, drink plenty of fluid, and eat nutritious food. Stay in a separate room from other family members. Medical treatments: If you have mild symptoms and are otherwise healthy, self-isolate and contact your medical provider or a COVID-19 information line for advice. Seek medical care if you have a fever, a cough, and difficulty breathing. Call in advance.

Prevention

To prevent the spread of COVID-19: Clean your hands often using handwash or sanitizer. Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
